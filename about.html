Our Story

Bringing the authentic taste of South Indian kitchens to your home, one pack at a time.

Welcome to Kaimanam

Kaimanam, meaning "the taste from hands" in Tamil, embodies our commitment to handcrafted, homemade South Indian food products. We believe that the best flavors come from traditional recipes prepared with love and care.

Our journey began with a simple mission: to share the authentic taste of grandmother's kitchen with the world. Every product we make is a tribute to the generations of women who perfected these recipes in their home kitchens.

We use only the finest organic ingredients, including cold-pressed gingelly oil (sesame oil), which has been a staple in South Indian cooking for centuries. No artificial preservatives, no shortcuts – just pure, unadulterated taste.
