Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

📦 What is the shelf life of your products?

Our podis and pickles have a shelf life of 3-6 months when stored properly in a cool, dry place. Thokku and mixes should be consumed within 2-3 months for best taste.

🚚 Do you deliver across India?

Yes! We deliver to all major cities and towns across India. Shipping charges are calculated based on your location and order size.

💰 What payment methods do you accept?

Currently, we accept Cash on Delivery (COD). Online payment options (UPI, Cards, Net Banking) via Razorpay will be available soon!

🛒 Can I place bulk orders for events or gifting?

Absolutely! We offer special pricing for bulk orders. Contact us on WhatsApp or email for custom requirements and bulk pricing.

🌿 Are your products really organic?

Yes! We use organic cold-pressed gingelly oil and source ingredients from trusted organic suppliers. No artificial preservatives or colors are used.

Authentic South Indian podis, mixes, and pickles made with organic ingredients and traditional recipes.
